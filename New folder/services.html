How It Works

From Idea to Final Frame

01

Discovery Call

We spend 60-90 minutes understanding your vision, audience, budget, and timeline. We ask the questions nobody else asks — about feeling, not just specs.

02

Concept & Mood

Our creative team produces an AI-generated mood board, storyboard frames, and a detailed prompt architecture — all for your approval before any rendering begins.

03

Production & Iteration

Full generation runs with weekly check-ins. You see rough cuts as we build. We iterate fast — each feedback loop typically completed within 48 hours.

04

Delivery & Mastering

Final master delivery in all formats: 4K DCP for cinema, broadcast ProRes, and web-optimized H.264/H.265. Full asset library included. You own everything.
