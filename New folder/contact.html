FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a project take?

A 60-second AI film typically takes 3–4 weeks from brief to delivery. Larger productions (3–12 minutes) run 6–10 weeks. We also offer an expedited track for time-sensitive projects.

Do I need to supply footage or assets?

No — we generate everything from scratch. That said, if you have brand assets, visual references, or existing footage you'd like incorporated, we can absolutely work with those.

Who owns the final film?

You do. On all paid projects you receive full commercial ownership and rights to the final deliverable and all master assets. We retain portfolio rights to showcase the work unless agreed otherwise.

How do I know if my email is genuinely from VisualNerds?

All official VisualNerds communications come exclusively from @visualnerds.com addresses. We never email from .co.uk, .net, or any other domain. If in doubt, see our official sites page or email us directly to verify.
