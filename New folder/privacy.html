Privacy Policy

We keep this short and plain. No legalese. Just what we actually do with your data.

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

VisualNerds is an AI film studio based near London, UK. Our website is visualnerds.com. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@visualnerds.com.

What Data We Collect

We collect minimal data. Specifically:

  • Email correspondence — when you email us at support@visualnerds.com, we receive and store that email and any information you include in it.
  • Basic website analytics — if we use analytics tools, we collect anonymised data such as page visits and browser type. We do not collect personally identifiable information through analytics.

We do not run any server-side contact forms that store your data in a database. Direct email is our only contact method.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information you send us solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or project request
  • Manage our ongoing working relationship if you become a client
  • Send you information you have specifically requested

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — we load fonts (Space Grotesk, Inter) from Google's servers. Google may log your IP address when serving these fonts. See Google's privacy policy for details.
  • YouTube — our showcase links to YouTube (youtube.com), which is operated by Google. Visiting YouTube is subject to their own privacy policy.

Cookies

This website does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party services (Google Fonts) may set their own cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Retention

Email correspondence is retained for as long as is reasonably necessary for business purposes. If you would like us to delete any correspondence or personal data we hold about you, email us at support@visualnerds.com and we will action your request promptly.

Your Rights (GDPR)

As a UK-based business, we comply with UK GDPR. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to our processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk) if you believe your rights have been breached

To exercise any of these rights, email support@visualnerds.com.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information communicated to us. Email is not an inherently secure channel — if you need to share sensitive information, contact us first so we can arrange a more secure method.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent update. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@visualnerds.com. We'll respond within 24 hours.
